Detailed Planets:

* this will involve a test where a planet is spawned, with a camera that allows rotation on two axes and zooming.
* it's meant to test LOD at different distances, especially as higher LOD becomes an option


Development Sequence:
	* increasing and decreasing sphere mesh detail
	* swapping between low and high res textures on approach
	* switching to divided spheres with multiple enhanced textures (and balancing memory load)
	* vertex deformation of the spheres to show terrain
	* generation of sections of higher detail terrain (w/ generation)
	* successively higher detail as camera approaches closely
	
	
Tools to work on:
	* sphere deformation
		* modifying vertices on a sphere to represent terrain
	* patch generation
		* variable size and vertex detail
		* variable curvature, ranging from highly curved to completely flat
	* subdividing a patch
		* increase detail level of an existing patch
